## Mission Statement

Our mission is to raise awareness of carbon footprints and inspire our community to take meaningful action for a healthier planet.

This initiative directly supports Sustainable Development Goals 11 (sustainable cities and communities) by promoting accessible and environmentally friendly transport options, as well as Goal 13 (climate action) by increasing awareness and encouraging reductions in carbon emissions.
