In 2015, a total of 10 teams competed in the App Challenge from schools participating in the ECCE program.

You can view all of their submissions in our GitHub repository for the challenge.

Winner:

The winner of the challenge in 2015 is the team UW TeamSDS, from the University of Waterloo. They build an app called PredictION, which aims to allow "governments to engage citizens in visualizing new transit routes, allowing citizens to analyze the routes' impact on travel times and service areas, and to compare new routes with the existing system" for the Grand Rivers Transit network in the Regional Municipality of Waterloo.

You can access their complete entry in our GitHub repo, as well as watch the video presentation that was submitted with their app.
